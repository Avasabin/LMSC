Become Agent

To become an Education Agent for La Maison Scholar College, you have the opportunity to partner with an esteemed institution committed to shaping futures and inspiring excellence. LMSC offers a range of courses in hospitality, information technology, and management, providing quality education and practical skills to students. As an agent, you'll assist in recruiting students, guiding them through the enrolment process, and promoting the college's programs. Join us in fostering academic growth and career success for students worldwide.
