    Frequently Asked Questions

    What programs does La Maison Scholar College offer?

    La Maison Scholar College offers a diverse range of programs across various disciplines, including Hospitality Management, Information Technology, Business Management, and more. Visit our website for a comprehensive list of programs.

    How can I apply to La Maison Scholar College?

    You can apply to La Maison Scholar College by visiting our website and completing the online application form. Alternatively, you can contact our admissions office for assistance with the application process.

    What are the admission requirements for undergraduate programs?

    Admission requirements vary depending on the program you are applying for. Generally, undergraduate applicants need to submit high school transcripts, standardized test scores (if applicable), letters of recommendation, and a personal statement. Additional requirements may apply to specific programs.
